📚 About Sign Languages

Sign languages are complete, natural languages with their own grammar and syntax. Each country or region typically has its own sign language. This tool provides fingerspelling representations for educational purposes.

Supported Sign Languages:

  • ASL - Widely used in the USA and Canada
  • BSL - Used in the United Kingdom
  • Auslan - Australian Sign Language
  • ISL - Indian Sign Language
  • JSL - Japanese Sign Language
  • And 5 more regional sign languages
